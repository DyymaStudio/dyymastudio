The Third and Last Experimental build of ACHSR v.2.0, Experimental-3, is releasing on May 16th.

This update continues the development of v2.0, primarily focusing on improving major systems for further usage, alongside setting a new standard for the future of ACHSR!

This update's highlight is the rework of the final level of Story Mode : Final Era! An entirely brand new intro has been added, giving proper build up to Emily's uphill battle against William! The level plays more chaotically now, along with an improved ending for a fitting climax.

Teachers have also been updated, having completely redone behavior that makes them all function much more consistently, with some very needed fixes (looking at you, Emma)!

In fact, there are fixes for Emma, as well as more changes and fixes for Sophie Adelbert, improving her overall behavior.

Experimental-3 is another step forward for ACHSR v.2.0, focused on fixing issues and improving gameplay quality.

As always, Experimental-2 will be replaced by Experimental-3, and this build will be locked behind a password available on our socials, including YouTube and Discord.

Thank you for supporting the project and helping test these experimental builds, we are excited to release the full version in June!
