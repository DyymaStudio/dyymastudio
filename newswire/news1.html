Angelica’s Chaotic Hardcore School is receiving a major update with v.1.3.0, launching on May 11th.

This update introduces:

  • New Bonus Level: "Computer Science" – The first bonus level, featuring tech-based challenges.
  • Fire Alarm System – The fire alarm will only activate if a call point is pressed, triggering a false alarm.
  • 60 New Language Sentences – Expanded dialogue for more interactions.
  • Free Mode Season 1 – The first seasonal update for Free Mode, bringing new content to explore.

ACHSR continues to expand with new features and challenges. Stay tuned for more updates.
