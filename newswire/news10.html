The second Experimental build of ACHSR v.2.0, called Experimental-2, will be released on Saturday, March 28th, 2026.

This update continues the development of v.2.0 and introduces several changes and improvements. One of the main additions is the New 3D Main Menu, which replaces the previous 2D Television Menu and gives the game a more modern and dynamic start.

Sophie Adelbert has also received some adjustments, improving how she behaves during gameplay. along with this, the update includes various bug fixes to make the experience smoother.

Another important change is that the Language Level layout has now been completed, improving how that level is structured and presented.

This build also introduces changes to the notebooks, updating how they appear and work during the game. in addition, the ending of the Math Level has been altered, bringing a different conclusion to that part of the game.

Experimental-2 is another step forward for ACHSR v.2.0, and more updates will come as development continues till the full version releases. Thank you for supporting the project and helping test these experimental builds.

Just to remind everyone, Experimental-1 will be replaced by Experimental-2 and this build is locked behind a password, which is available on our socials Youtube and Discord.
