In an unexpected announcement, we have decided to cancel Free Mode, which allowed players to roam freely outside the main school, will no longer be part of the game’s future updates.

This decision was made to focus entirely on the game’s lore and main horror gameplay. Free Mode no longer aligns with the direction we’re taking, We want every part of the game to feel meaningful, intense, and directly tied to Angelica’s Universe.

To make up for the cancellation, the long-anticipated V.1.3.0 update has been brought forward to April 11th, 2025. The update promises major story developments, new chaotic encounters, and key improvements that will shape the future of the game.

Players are encouraged to update their game on April 11th to dive into the First Bonus Level (which will be free to play) of Angelica’s Chaotic Hardcore School.

We sincerely apologize for this decision, but we believe it was the most appropriate course of action for the future of the game.

-The DyymaStudio Team
