The next major update for Angelica's Chaotic Hardcore School Remastered, version 1.4.0, will now release earlier than expected on July 5th. The announcement came alongside the release of a second teaser trailer, giving players a brief look at what’s coming.

One of the highlights is the introduction of a new teacher, Flaggerong Flagensky, whose movements closely mirror those of Angelica Wilkins. The update is expected to bring more detailed story content, such as the story introduction and the intro game cutscene. And more.
More details will be revealed as launch day approaches.
