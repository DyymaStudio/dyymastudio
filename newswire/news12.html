This year marks 5 years of Angelica's Chaotic Hardcore School.

It's hard to believe we are now five years through development, updates, redesigns, experimentation, and chaos. A game that started out as nothing more than a fan-game for Advanced Education with Viktor Strobovski, has now grown into a game sold on Steam.

With that in mind, we would like to celebrate this milestone with the announcement of the ACHSR 5th Anniversary video. This video was created as a tribute to the game's history, revisiting the past half-decade of the game's history, moving across past versions, memories, and videos from people who played the game.

The trailer also serves to mark the official conclusion of Era 1, bringing the first chapter of ACHSR's history to a close, and marking the end of an era that defined the project's foundation.

The 5th Anniversary Trailer will premiere on June 20, 2026 at 22:00 CEST (4 PM ET).

This video is dedicated to everyone who supported ACHSR over the years, whether its new players, content creators, community members, and even people who followed development from the very start. We'd like to thank you for helping us keep the chaos alive.

As one era comes to an end, another one always begins; the future of ACHSR awaits.
